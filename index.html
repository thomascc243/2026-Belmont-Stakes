The Formula

How value scores are built

The formula identifies horses the public is systematically under-betting in exotic pools relative to their actual form. Scarcity gets the highest weight — market inefficiency lives there. Post position is factored in because it matters enormously in a 20-horse field.

Value Score = (Implied Probability × 0.35) + (Pool Scarcity × 0.40) + (Payout Multiplier × 0.25) + (Post Position Bonus × 0.05)
Implied Probability
Win probability from current odds: 1 ÷ (odds+1) × 100
Pool Scarcity
How under-bet in exotic pools: 100 − estimated pool inclusion %
Payout Multiplier
Odds-based return potential — sweet spot 10-1 to 30-1
Post Position
+5 for posts 5–15 · −5 for posts 1–3 and 17+ · 0 for 16–17
