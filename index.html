The Formula

How value scores are calculated

Value Score = (Implied Probability × 0.35) + (Pool Scarcity × 0.40) + (Payout Multiplier × 0.25)
Implied Probability — win probability from current odds (1 ÷ (odds+1) × 100)
Pool Scarcity — how under-bet the horse is in exotic pools (100 − pool %)
Payout Multiplier — odds-based return potential
Key insight — scarcity gets the highest weight because market inefficiency lives there

Pace context: Three front-runners (Ezum, Exhibition Only, Corona de Oro) create a contested early pace. The formula boosts Confessional as the primary beneficiary — proven stalker, highest Brisnet (95), Keeneland winner, Irad Ortiz Jr. aboard.
